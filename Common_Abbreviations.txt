These are some common abbreviations I use in my code:

doff: Dynamic Offline
soff: Static Offline

obs: Observations
ave: Average
inst: Instantaneous

curr: Current
prev: Previous

mean: A mean
dev: Deviations from a mean
var: Variance

nX: The number of Xs
Xdex: The indices of Xs

t: Time
tSpan: Length of an averaging period
ens: Ensemble
N: Length of a state vector
